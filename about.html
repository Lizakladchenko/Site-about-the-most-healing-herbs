About me

My name is Kladchenko Liza and I am a full-time blogger and author of a blog tutorial called Flowers

I have a lot of favorite hobbies. I love to read, I like to ride a bicycle, and I really like the story. But my main hobby is painting. I started drawing when I was still small. Mom and Dad very often gave me felt-tip pens of various colors, colored pencils and beautiful, snow-white paper for drawing.

I also really love animals. My favorite animal is a dog. Honestly, I really love all animals. I like cats, and hamsters, and fish, and birds, and even domestic rats, but the dog is my favorite animal. As they say, a dog is a man’s best friend. And I completely agree with that. Dogs bring happiness and joy to humans. They are always very funny, they always want to frolic and play something, whether it be a ball or a simple bone. They protect the human house from strangers, protect their territory. It seems to me that dogs are very loyal to man, to their master. You can recall the legendary story of the dog Hachiko, who was left to wait for his long-dead master. Hachiko is the ideal of a faithful, faithful dog.

Thanks for visiting!
